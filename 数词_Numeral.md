数字 Number:

### 设计哲理 Philosophy:

1. The numbers 0-10 are all one syllable long [音节 Syllable](%E9%9F%B3%E8%8A%82_Syllable.md) (instead of "se-ven" in English) so that they are easier to be pronounced & remembered (eg: (eg: in multiplication table & calculation).

2. The numbers 1-10 follows a sequence from `a` to `u`.

3. Odd numbers end with `n` (single door).

4. Even numbers end with `m` (double door).
