## 音节 Syllable

Any word with shorter syllable (4 syllable or less) is easier to remember & pronounce. Thus, shorter syllable word should be used for most commonly used word.

---

The 1-syllable (single syllable)

- `wa`

- `chi`

---

The 1.5-syllable

- `wai`

- `chio`

---

The 2- syllable

- `wawa`

- `beti`

---

The 2.5 syllable

- `wangda`

---

The 3 syllable

- `anata`

---

The 3.5 syllable

- `remember`

---

The 4-syllable

- `wangbadan`

---

The 4.5-syllable

- `relationship`
